# FLOWFIT KIDS: COMPLETE ONE-PARAGRAPH SUMMARY (WITH PARENT MONITORING)

## 🎯 Project Brief

FlowFit Kids transforms our adult fitness app into a child-friendly version (ages 7-12) featuring a single customizable pet companion called "Buddy" that grows with the child's fitness journey. The onboarding is super simple: kids immediately meet Buddy, choose their favorite color from 10 options (red, blue, green, etc.), name their pet, and start their first workout challenge—all in under 90 seconds without any parent involvement upfront. As kids complete workouts and hit milestones, they earn XP to level up Buddy (evolving through 5 stages from Baby to Mega Buddy), unlock new colors (starting with 3 free colors, unlocking more at levels 3, 5, 8, etc.), and collect accessories like caps, capes, and shoes that they can mix and match in a simple dress-up screen. Parents can link to their child's account anytime using a 6-digit code (prompted after 3 days of use or when the child tries to access certain features) to access a comprehensive parent dashboard on the phone app that shows each child's weekly activity summary, average exercise minutes per day, sleep duration, heart rate status, Buddy's current level, completed workouts, and achievement milestones—plus parental controls to set daily watch screen time limits, adjust activity goals, manage privacy settings, enable/disable features, receive notifications for important milestones or concerns, export health reports for pediatricians, and even send motivational messages or encouragement directly to the child's watch when they need a boost. The watch tracks heart rate and activity (simplified as "Your pet is happy!" instead of technical BPM zones), syncs data to both the child's simple pet-focused interface and the parent's detailed analytics dashboard, and everything uses large buttons, bright colors, minimal text, and fun animations to keep 7-12 year olds engaged without overwhelming them with complex fitness metrics while giving parents complete visibility and control over their child's health journey.

## 📊 Parent Dashboard Quick Reference

**What Parents Can See:**
- Weekly activity summaries per child
- Daily exercise minutes, steps, heart rate trends
- Sleep duration and quality
- Buddy level progression and achievements
- Workout completion history
- Nutrition logs (if child uses food tracker)

**What Parents Can Control:**
- Screen time limits (e.g., max 2 hours/day on watch)
- Activity goals (e.g., 30 min exercise/day)
- Privacy settings (data sharing, who can see profile)
- Feature permissions (which app sections child can access)
- Notification preferences (get alerts for milestones/concerns)
- Emergency contact setup (quick access from watch)

**What Parents Can Do:**
- Link multiple children to one parent account
- Compare progress across siblings (optional)
- Send encouragement messages to child's watch
- Export health reports (PDF for doctors)
- Review and delete child's data anytime
- Set quiet hours (no notifications during school/sleep)
